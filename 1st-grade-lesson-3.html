Lesson 3: Comprehension Peak

Understanding what you read!

Great job making it this far! Now comes the best part - understanding stories! When you read, you need to think about what's happening. Let me tell you a story!

üìñ Billy's Adventure

Billy's Big Day

It was early in the morning. Billy the goat woke up excited. Today he was going to climb a new peak!

Billy packed his bag. He put in some water and snacks. Then he started walking up the mountain.

The climb was hard. Billy's legs were tired. But he did not give up. He kept climbing higher and higher.

At the top, Billy rested under a big tree. He could see the whole valley below. Billy felt proud and happy.

"I did it!" Billy said with a big smile.

üéØ Comprehension Questions

WHO and WHAT Questions:

  1. Who is the main character? (Billy the goat)
  2. What did Billy want to do? (Climb a new peak)
  3. What did Billy pack? (Water and snacks)

WHERE and WHEN Questions:

  1. Where did Billy rest? (Under a big tree)
  2. When did Billy start his climb? (Early in the morning)
  3. Where could Billy see the whole valley? (At the top)

WHY and HOW Questions:

  1. Why was Billy excited? (He was going to climb a new peak)
  2. How did Billy feel at the top? (Proud and happy)

üéÆ Practice Game: Story Words!

Great job understanding my story! When you read, always ask yourself: Who is in the story? What happens? Where and when does it happen? That helps you climb to the top of understanding!
