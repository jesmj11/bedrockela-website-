📖 Ann's Jam

Ann has jam in a can.

The jam is red.

Ann ran to Sam.

"Sam! Sam!" said Ann.

"I have jam in a can!"

Sam ran to Ann.

"Can I have the jam?" said Sam.

"Yes!" said Ann.

Sam and Ann had the jam.

The jam was good!
