📖 Max and the Hat

Max the cat had a hat.

The hat was on the mat.

A bag sat by the hat.

Max ran and sat on the bag.

"The bag is flat!" said Max.

Max got the hat and sat on the mat.

"The mat is not flat," said Max.

Max had a nap on the mat with the hat.
