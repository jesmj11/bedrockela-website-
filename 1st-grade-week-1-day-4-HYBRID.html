📖 Dan's Bag

Dan has a big bag.

In the bag is a map and a cap.

Dan ran to Dad with the bag.

"Dad! I have a map!" said Dan.

Dad and Dan sat on the mat.

They had the map on the lap.

"The map is good!" said Dad.

Dan had the cap on his head.

Dan and Dad are glad.
