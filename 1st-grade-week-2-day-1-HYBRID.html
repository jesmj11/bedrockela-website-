📖 Ben's Red Net (Part 1)

Ben had a red net.

He set the net by the pond.

A wet hen sat on a log and pecked.

📖 Ben's Red Net (Part 2)

Ben saw the hen and got a pet peg toy to play.

He let the pet peg sit in the net.

A small red bug met the net and ran.

📖 Ben's Red Net (Part 3)

Ben fed the hen with a bit of bread.

The hen pecked and then sat on Ben's lap.

Ben felt glad.
