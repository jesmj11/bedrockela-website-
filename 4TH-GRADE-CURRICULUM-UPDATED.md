## 🎯 Why Three Musketeers Works Better Than Call of the Wild

### Call of the Wild Challenges:
- Heavy themes (death, survival, wildness) can be intense for 4th grade
- Dog protagonist less relatable than human heroes
- Shorter book needed heavy padding with Jungle Book

### Three Musketeers Advantages:
- ✅ Human characters (relatable)
- ✅ Friendship theme (classroom community focus)
- ✅ Adventure and humor balance serious moments
- ✅ Perfect length: 24 chapters = clean 30-lesson unit
- ✅ Historical setting (17th century France) teaches culture/history
- ✅ Iconic motto "All for one, one for all!" becomes class motto
- ✅ Age-appropriate adaptation already complete
