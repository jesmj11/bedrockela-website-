📖 Part 2: Today’s Task

No new reading. This is a vocabulary review day!


✅ Check Your Understanding

Answer these questions using what you’ve learned.


1. Which word means “a fraud or fake”?

A) humbug

B) captive

C) narrative

D) labyrinth


2. Which word means “a complicated maze”?

A) quest

B) labyrinth

C) cyclone

D) climax


3. Which word means “the most exciting point of a story”?

A) dialogue

B) expression

C) climax

D) narrative


4. Which word means “specific details from a text that support your point”?

A) feedback

B) evidence

C) reflection

D) portfolio


5. Pick 3 vocabulary words from the list above and use EACH ONE in a sentence of your own (not copied from the lessons). Your sentences should show you understand what the word means.

💡 Hint: Choose words you actually like and might use in real life!

6. Which 5 vocabulary words from this unit are your favorites? Why? Which ones do you want to keep using in your own writing and speaking?

💡 Hint: Think about which words sound cool, are useful, or helped you understand a big idea.

[Your answer]
