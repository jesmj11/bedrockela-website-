🧐 Part 1: Before You Read

Before you do anything else, close your eyes for 10 seconds and think about your favorite moment from the last 30 days. Was it a scene from the book? Something you wrote? A word you learned? A question that made you think? Hold onto that moment. That’s what reading does — it gives you moments that stay with you.


📖 Part 2: Today’s Task

No reading assignment today. Today is about YOU and your growth.


💬 Part 3: Word of the Day

transformation (noun)

A major change. When something or someone becomes something new and different.


From the story: Dorothy went through a transformation on her journey. She started as a scared girl and became a brave leader. You’ve been through a transformation too — in just 30 days.


Think about it: How have YOU been transformed as a reader and writer? What can you do now that you couldn’t do on Day 1?


✍️ Part 4: Letter to Yourself + Looking Ahead

Write a letter to yourself about Unit 1. Be honest, be proud, and be specific.


Dear Me,


Write about:

  • What I’m most proud of from Unit 1
  • The hardest thing I did and how I got through it
  • My favorite book/myth/story moment
  • How I’ve grown as a reader and writer
  • What I’m excited about for Unit 2

[Write your letter here]
