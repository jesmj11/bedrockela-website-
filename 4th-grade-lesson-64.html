üßê Part 1: Before You Read

Stories have different TYPES of conflict:

  • Character vs. Character (Buck vs. Spitz)
  • Character vs. Nature (Buck vs. the Yukon)
  • Character vs. Self (Buck vs. his own civilized instincts)
  • Character vs. Society (Buck vs. the humans who control him)

The Call of the Wild has ALL of these. Today, character vs. character takes center stage.


Think about this: In nature, leadership is determined by strength and intelligence. In human society, it is more complicated. Which system is more fair?

üí¨ Part 3: Word of the Day

dominance (noun)

Power and control over others. In nature, dominance determines who leads, who eats first, and who survives.


From the story: Buck‚Äôs fight with Spitz is a fight for dominance ‚Äî the winner becomes the leader of the pack.


Think about it: Is dominance the same as leadership? Can someone be dominant without being a good leader?
