üìñ Part 2: Today‚Äôs Task

Read Kaa‚Äôs Hunting. Mowgli is kidnapped by the Bandar-log (monkeys) who have no law and no discipline. Baloo, Bagheera, and the python Kaa must rescue him.


Reading tip: The monkeys represent chaos ‚Äî they have no law, no structure, no respect for rules. Kipling is contrasting the disciplined jungle (wolves, bears, panthers) with the chaotic jungle (monkeys). This is another layer of the civilization theme.

üí¨ Part 3: Word of the Day

discipline (noun)

Training and rules that develop self-control and orderly behavior. The ability to follow structure even when it is hard.


From the story: Baloo teaches Mowgli discipline through the Law of the Jungle. The monkeys have no discipline, which is why the other animals despise them.


Think about it: Is discipline a civilized quality or a natural one? The wolves have discipline. The monkeys do not. What does that tell you?
