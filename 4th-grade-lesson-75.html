üßê Part 1: Before You Read

Transformation paragraph tips:

  • Show the character BEFORE and AFTER the setting changes them
  • Use specific moments or scenes as evidence
  • Explain HOW the setting caused the change (not just that it happened)

Meaning paragraph tips:

  • Connect to the theme of nature vs. civilization
  • What does this transformation TEACH us?
  • This is where you show your thinking, not just your describing

Conclusion tips:

  • Echo your introduction
  • End with a memorable final line
  • Leave the reader with something to think about
