üßê Part 1: Before You Read

This essay has TWO kinds of writing that need revision:


Descriptive writing revision:

  • Can the reader SEE, HEAR, FEEL your setting?
  • Did you use at least one simile and one metaphor?
  • Are your details specific or vague?

Vague: The place was scary. ‚Üí Vivid: The silence pressed against my ears like a hand.


Analytical writing revision:

  • Did you explain HOW the setting changes the character?
  • Did you say WHY it matters (connect to theme)?
  • Are you THINKING on the page, or just summarizing?

Summary: Buck changed in the Yukon. ‚Üí Analysis: The Yukon stripped away everything civilized about Buck, revealing an ancient wildness that had been sleeping inside him all along.


üìñ Part 2: Today‚Äôs Task

Re-read your entire essay. Read it OUT LOUD. Mark places where the description feels flat or the analysis feels thin.


The best revision trick for descriptive writing: close your eyes after reading each sentence. Can you picture it? If not, the description needs work.
