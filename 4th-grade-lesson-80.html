üí¨ Part 3: Word of the Day

synthesis (noun)

Combining multiple ideas or sources into a unified understanding. When you put Buck and Mowgli together and draw a BIGGER conclusion, that is synthesis.


From the story: Synthesis means going beyond comparing two books to finding the BIGGER idea they share. Buck and Mowgli tell opposite stories, but together they teach us that everyone must answer the question: where do I belong?


Think about it: How is synthesis different from just comparing? Comparison shows similarities and differences. Synthesis finds the DEEPER MEANING that emerges when you put them together.
