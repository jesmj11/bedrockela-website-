üí¨ Part 3: Word of the Day

thesis (noun)

The main argument or central idea of an essay. Your thesis is the claim you are making that the rest of your essay supports.


From the story: A strong thesis for this essay might be: The Call of the Wild and The Jungle Book explore nature vs. civilization from opposite directions, but together they reveal that true identity comes from within, not from where you live.
