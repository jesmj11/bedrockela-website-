# 5th Grade Curriculum Structure
**180 Days Total** (144 regular lessons + 36 assessments)

## Assessment Pattern
- Every 5th day = Assessment (vocab quiz + comprehension test)
- Regular days: 11 pages
- Assessment days: 8 pages

## Vocabulary Standards
- **5th Grade**: 3 words per day from actual text
- Assessment: 12-word quiz every 5th day (4 days × 3 words)

---

## 11-Page Structure (Regular Days)
1. Title & Welcome
2. Vocabulary (3 words from text)
3. Vocab Practice Game (cumulative review)
4-6. Story (3 parts, no scrolling)
7. Reading Comprehension (3 questions)
8. Grammar OR Language (alternating)
9. Informational Text + Questions
10. Writing Skills OR Journal (alternating)
11. Completion

## 8-Page Structure (Assessment Days)
1. Title & Welcome
2. Vocabulary Quiz (12 words)
3. Reading Comprehension Test
4. Grammar Review
5. Language Review
6. Writing Prompt
7. Review Game
8. Completion
