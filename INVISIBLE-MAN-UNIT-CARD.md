## 📚 Story Overview

A modern adaptation of H.G. Wells' psychological thriller. Brilliant scientist Griffin discovers the secret of invisibility, but the power corrupts him. As he descends into madness, he becomes a threat to society. Themes of ambition, isolation, moral responsibility, and the dangers of unchecked power.

**Setting:** Present day England (Iping village and London)  
**Protagonist:** Griffin (30s, brilliant but unstable scientist)  
**Supporting Cast:**
- Mrs. Hall - Village innkeeper
- Thomas Marvel - Reluctant accomplice
- Dr. Kemp - Griffin's former colleague

**Arc:** From ambitious scientist → isolated genius → dangerous villain → tragic downfall
