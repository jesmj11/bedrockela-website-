## 📚 UNIT OVERVIEW

**Theme:** Justice vs. Vengeance  
**Central Question:** When does justice become revenge, and when does revenge destroy the avenger?

**Why This Story:**
- The ultimate revenge tale - but with moral complexity
- Shows transformation from innocent victim to powerful avenger to merciful sage
- Explores themes of patience, planning, wealth, power, and forgiveness
- Perfect capstone for 8th grade - synthesizes all year's themes

## 📖 CHAPTER BREAKDOWN (2 Chapters Per Day)

### Week 34: BETRAYAL AND IMPRISONMENT (Days 166-170)

**Day 166: Chapters 1-2 - "Happiness and Betrayal"**
- Ch 1: The Treasure Isle - Introduction to Edmond Dantès, successful young sailor
- Ch 2: The Betrayal of Innocence - False accusations of treason
- **Vocabulary:** treasure, mysterious, legendary, agenda, wisdom, betrayal, innocence, captain, promotion, accusations
- **Theme:** Dramatic irony - reader sees the conspiracy Dantès cannot
- **Discussion:** How does success breed jealousy?

**Day 167: Chapters 3-4 - "Conspiracy and Arrest"**
- Ch 3: The Web of Conspiracy - Fernand, Danglars, and Villefort plot
- Ch 4: The Arrest - Dantès arrested on his wedding day
- **Vocabulary:** conspiracy, jealous, envious, vindictive, anonymous, arrest, prosecutor, sacrifice, career, warrant
- **Theme:** Multiple motivations for evil (love, money, career)
- **Discussion:** Can good people do terrible things for "good" reasons?

**Day 168: Chapters 5-6 - "Prison and Despair"**
- Ch 5: The Château d'If - The fortress prison on an island
- Ch 6: Years of Despair - Slow descent into hopelessness
- **Vocabulary:** château, fortress, prisoners, bureaucratic, indifference, despair, gradual, deterioration, anguish, confinement
- **Theme:** Unjust imprisonment destroys hope
- **Discussion:** How would you maintain sanity in solitary confinement?

**Day 169: Chapters 7-8 - "Hope Returns"**
- Ch 7: The Abbé's Voice - Meeting Abbé Faria through the walls
- Ch 8: The University of Suffering - Education and transformation in prison
- **Vocabulary:** abbé, scratching, fellow, tunnel, noble, systematic, protégé, mathematics, philosophy, strategy
- **Theme:** Education as power, mentorship, hope
- **Discussion:** How does learning change Dantès?

**Day 170: ASSESSMENT #1 - Week 34 Review**
- Vocabulary Quiz: 40 words (Chapters 1-8)
- Comprehension: Betrayal and imprisonment section
- Writing: "Was Villefort the worst villain? Explain why or why not."

---

### Week 35: ESCAPE AND TRANSFORMATION (Days 171-175)

**Day 171: Chapters 9-10 - "Treasure and Rebirth"**
- Ch 9: The Count's Treasure - Faria reveals treasure location on Monte Cristo island
- Ch 10: Death and Rebirth - Faria dies, Dantès escapes in body bag
- **Vocabulary:** revelation, massive, instructions, claiming, unlimited, rebirth, switching, corpse, dramatic, emerged
- **Theme:** Death of old self, birth of avenger
- **Discussion:** What does the treasure symbolize?

**Day 172: Chapters 11-12 - "Discovery and Planning"**
- Ch 11: The Treasure of Knowledge - Dantès finds the treasure
- Ch 12: Creating the Count - Transformation into mysterious nobleman
- **Vocabulary:** inheritance, discovery, counting, combined, systematic, metamorphosis, preparation, network, allies, influence
- **Theme:** Wealth as power, planning revenge
- **Discussion:** How does money change a person?

**Day 173: Chapters 13-14 - "Return to Paris"**
- Ch 13: The Return - Count of Monte Cristo arrives in Paris society
- Ch 14: Identifying the Enemies - Finding those who betrayed him
- **Vocabulary:** aristocrat, mysterious, lavish, extravagant, impressed, reconnaissance, subtle, gathering, observing, patience
- **Theme:** The long game of revenge
- **Discussion:** Why doesn't the Count reveal himself?

**Day 174: Chapters 15-16 - "Connections and Manipulation"**
- Ch 15: Winning Friends - Building relationships with key people
- Ch 16: The Web Forms - Positioning pieces for revenge
- **Vocabulary:** charismatic, generous, fascinating, strategic, manipulation, orchestrating, complexity, interconnected, subtle, precision
- **Theme:** Manipulation vs. friendship
- **Discussion:** Are the Count's new "friends" real or tools?

**Day 175: ASSESSMENT #2 - Week 35 Review**
- Vocabulary Quiz: 40 words (Chapters 9-16)
- Comprehension: Escape and transformation section
- Writing: "How has prison changed Edmond Dantès? Compare his personality before and after."

### Week 36: JUSTICE AND REDEMPTION (Days 176-180)

**Day 176: Chapters 17-18 - "Revenge Begins"**
- Ch 17: Fernand's Downfall - Exposing military betrayal

- **Vocabulary:** exposure, revelation, disgrace, honor, military, speculation, financial, bankruptcy, ruin, collapse

**Day 177: Chapters 19-20 - "Innocent Victims"**

- **Vocabulary:** consequences, innocent, victims, madness, unintended, realization, horror, guilt, responsibility, mercy

- **Discussion:** When does justice become cruelty?

**Day 178: Chapters 21-22 - "Love and Forgiveness"**
- Ch 21: Mercédès Pleads - Former love begs for mercy
- Ch 22: Valentine Saved - Choosing mercy over revenge
- **Vocabulary:** forgiveness, mercy, compassion, love, sacrifice, redemption, choice, humanity, transformation, grace
- **Theme:** Love redeems, mercy triumphs
- **Discussion:** What makes the Count spare Valentine?

**Day 179: Chapters 23-24 - "Departure and Wisdom"**
- Ch 23: Final Encounters - Saying goodbye to friends and enemies
- Ch 24: "Wait and Hope" - The Count's final wisdom
- **Vocabulary:** wisdom, patience, hope, future, peace, resolution, departure, legacy, philosophy, redemption
