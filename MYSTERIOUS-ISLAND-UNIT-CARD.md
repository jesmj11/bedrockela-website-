## 📚 Story Overview

A modern adaptation of Jules Verne's engineering masterpiece. Five castaways escape the Civil War in a hot air balloon, crash on a remote island, and must rebuild civilization from nothing using science and ingenuity. Themes of teamwork, problem-solving, innovation, and the mysterious benefactor who helps them in secret (Captain Nemo).

**Setting:** Remote Pacific island, present day  
**Protagonists:** 
- Cyrus Harding - Brilliant engineer
- Gideon Spilett - Journalist and chronicler
- Neb - Loyal friend and cook
- Herbert Brown - Young naturalist
- Pencroft - Sailor and practical expert

**Arc:** From desperate survivors → resourceful builders → discovering the island's secrets

## 📊 Assessment Structure

### Day 50 Assessment (Week 10)
- **Vocabulary Quiz:** Words 1-12
- **Grammar/Language:** Technical writing, Greek/Latin roots
- **Comprehension:** Synthesize chapters 1-8
- **Writing Prompt:** "What makes a good survivor? Use examples from the story and real survival situations."

### Day 55 Assessment (Week 11)
- **Vocabulary Quiz:** Words 13-24
- **Grammar/Language:** Parallel structure, cause-effect
- **Comprehension:** Synthesize chapters 9-16
- **Writing Prompt:** "How does engineering knowledge help humans overcome natural challenges? Support with evidence."

### Day 60 Final Assessment (Week 12)
- **Vocabulary Quiz:** Words 25-36 + review
- **Grammar/Language:** All week 12 topics + review
- **Comprehension:** Unit synthesis
- **Writing Prompt:** "What is the value of scientific knowledge in survival situations? Use evidence from The Mysterious Island and the informational articles."
