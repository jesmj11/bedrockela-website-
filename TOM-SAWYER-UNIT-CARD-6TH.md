# Tom Sawyer Unit Card - 6th Grade

**Book:** The Adventures of Tom Sawyer (BedrockELA Adaptation)  
**Author:** Mark Twain (Adapted for 6th Grade)  
**Grade Level:** 6th  
**Unit Length:** 6 weeks (30 days)  
**Chapters:** 24 adapted chapters  

---

## 📚 Unit Overview

Students follow mischievous Tom Sawyer through adventures in 1840s Missouri: whitewashing fences, witnessing murder, running away to be pirates, appearing at his own funeral, falling in love with Becky Thatcher, testifying at a trial, hunting treasure, and getting lost in a cave.

**Themes:**
- Growing up and taking responsibility
- Friendship and loyalty
- Justice vs. revenge
- Childhood imagination vs. adult expectations
- Small-town American life in the 1800s

---

## 📖 Chapter Breakdown (24 Chapters)

### Week 1 (Days 1-5)
1. **Tom and Aunt Polly** - Introduction to Tom's mischievous nature
2. **The Whitewashing** - Tom tricks friends into whitewashing the fence
3. **Showing Off for Becky** - Tom meets Becky Thatcher
4. **The Engagement** - Tom and Becky's "engagement"
5. **Assessment**

### Week 2 (Days 6-10)
6. **The Graveyard** - Tom and Huck witness Injun Joe's murder
7. **The Blood Oath** - Boys swear to keep the secret
8. **The Town in Shock** - Aftermath of the murder
9. **Pirates of Jackson's Island** - Boys run away
10. **Assessment**

### Week 3 (Days 11-15)
11. **Life on the Island** - Pirate adventures
12. **Their Own Funeral** - Tom appears at his own funeral
13. **Tom's Dream Trick** - Tom's clever lie
14. **Making Up with Becky** - Tom takes Becky's punishment
15. **Assessment**

### Week 4 (Days 16-20)
16. **Examination Day** - School testing
17. **A Miserable Summer** - Tom's boring summer
18. **The Trial** - Tom testifies against Injun Joe
19. **Treasure Hunting** - Boys search for buried treasure
20. **Assessment**

### Week 5 (Days 21-25)
21. **The Haunted House** - Finding gold but losing it
22. **Tracking Number Two** - Following Injun Joe
23. **The Picnic** - School picnic and cave exploration
24. **Huck Saves the Widow** - Huck prevents robbery
25. **Assessment**

### Week 6 (Days 26-30)
26. **Lost in the Cave** - Tom and Becky trapped
27. **Escape** - Tom finds a way out
28. **Injun Joe's Fate** - Villain meets his end
29. **Tom Sawyer's Gang** - Tom plans future adventures
30. **Final Assessment**

## 📊 Assessment Structure (Days 5, 10, 15, 20, 25, 30)

**Each Friday Assessment Includes:**
1. Vocabulary Quiz (12 words from the week)
2. Grammar/Language Review (mixed questions)
3. Comprehension Questions (covering week's chapters)
4. Writing Prompt (summative task)

**Final Assessment (Day 30):**
- Comprehensive vocabulary (24 words from all 6 weeks)
- Grammar/Language cumulative review
- Novel comprehension (whole book questions)
- Final essay prompt (theme analysis or character study)
