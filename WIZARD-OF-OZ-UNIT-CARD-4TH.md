## 📖 Story Overview

Dorothy Gale is swept away from Kansas by a cyclone and lands in the magical Land of Oz. Along the yellow brick road, she befriends the Scarecrow (who wants a brain), the Tin Woodman (who wants a heart), and the Cowardly Lion (who wants courage). Together they journey to the Emerald City to ask the great Wizard of Oz for help, discovering along the way that they already possess what they seek.

**Key Characters:**
- **Dorothy** - Brave girl from Kansas
- **Toto** - Dorothy's loyal dog
- **Scarecrow** - Friend who thinks he needs a brain
- **Tin Woodman** - Friend who thinks he needs a heart
- **Cowardly Lion** - Friend who thinks he needs courage
- **Wizard of Oz** - Mysterious ruler of Emerald City
- **Glinda** - Good Witch of the South
- **Wicked Witch of the West** - Main antagonist

## ✍️ Writing Focus: LAUNCHING READING JOURNALS

**This is the FIRST unit of the year!**

**Days 1-5:** Introduce reading journals
- 2-3 sentences per day
- Simple prompts: "What happened?" "What surprised you?" "What do you predict?"
- Model on board, practice complete sentences

**Days 6-15:** Build to paragraphs
- 4-6 sentences
- Character descriptions with evidence
- Personal connections to the story

**Days 16-25:** Add complexity
- Full paragraph responses
- Compare/contrast characters
- Theme identification

**Days 26-30:** First narrative writing
- Students write their own hero's journey story
- Use Dorothy's journey as a model
- Focus: beginning, middle, end with character growth

## 🎮 Grammar & Language Skills

### Grammar Focus (Days 1, 3, 6, 8, 11, 13, 16, 18, 21, 23, 26, 28)
- Complete sentences vs. fragments
- Subjects and predicates
- Capitalizing proper nouns (Kansas, Oz, Dorothy)
- End punctuation (. ! ?)
- Compound sentences with "and," "but," "or"
- Action verbs (walked, jumped, melted)

### Language Skills (Days 2, 4, 7, 9, 12, 14, 17, 19, 22, 24, 27, 29)
- Context clues to figure out word meanings
- Vocabulary from the text
- Similes ("as brave as a lion")
- Color words and descriptive language
- Synonyms (brave/courageous, wicked/evil)
- Fantasy vs. reality

## 📋 Comprehension Questions Structure

**3 questions per lesson (mix of):**
- **Literal:** What happened in this chapter?
- **Inferential:** Why did the character feel that way?
- **Personal:** How would you react in that situation?
- **Character:** How is Dorothy brave?
- **Theme:** What lesson does this teach?

**Example Day 1:**
1. What happens to Dorothy's house during the cyclone?
2. How does Dorothy feel when she wakes up in Oz?
3. If you suddenly landed in a strange place, what would you do first?
