# 1st Grade ELA - REVISED Scope & Sequence (CCSS Aligned)
## BedrockELA Curriculum - Proper 1st Grade Level

**IMPORTANT CHANGE:** This curriculum assumes students enter 1st grade with:
- All letter names and sounds mastered (Kindergarten RF.K.3a)
- Ability to read simple CVC words (Kindergarten RF.K.2d)
- Basic print concepts (Kindergarten RF.K.1)

**Aligned with:**
- Common Core State Standards (CCSS) for 1st Grade ELA
- 180-day school year format
- Appropriate 1st grade rigor
