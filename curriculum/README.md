## ğŸŒ€ Spiral Learning Approach

Writing develops in 5 overlapping phases:

### **Phase 1:** Letters & Sounds (Lessons 1-20)
- Tracing and writing letters
- Phonics connection

### **Phase 2:** Letters Mastery (Lessons 15-40)
- All 26 letters mastered
- Labeling pictures

### **Phase 3:** Words (Lessons 35-75)
- Spelling CVC words
- Word families
- Sight words

### **Phase 4:** Sentences (Lessons 65-120)
- Complete sentences
- Capitals & punctuation
- 1-2 sentences

### **Phase 5:** Paragraphs (Lessons 100-180)
- Multiple connected sentences
- Topic sentences & details
- Full paragraphs

**Phases overlap intentionally** - that's the spiral! ğŸŒ€
