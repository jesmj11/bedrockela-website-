# Spiral Learning Map - 1st Grade BedrockELA
## Complete 180-Lesson Writing Progression
### Reading + Writing Integrated Daily

---

## Overview: The 5 Phases of Writing Development

| Phase | Lessons | Focus | Writing Output |
|-------|---------|-------|----------------|
| **Phase 1: Reading Foundation** | 1-20 | Letters & Sounds | Letter formation, tracing |
| **Phase 2: Letters & Sounds Mastery** | 15-40 | All letters + phonics | Writing all letters, labeling |
| **Phase 3: Words** | 35-75 | CVC words, sight words | Spelling words, word families |
| **Phase 4: Phrases & Sentences** | 65-120 | Simple sentences | 1 complete sentence |
| **Phase 5: Multiple Sentences** | 100-180 | Connected ideas | 2-3+ sentences, paragraphs |

**Note:** Phases OVERLAP intentionally! That's the spiral! üåÄ

## Phase 1: Reading Foundation (Lessons 1-20)

### Writing Focus: Letter Formation & Phonics Connection

**What Students Learn:**
- How to hold a pencil/crayon correctly
- Letter formation (uppercase and lowercase)
- Letter-sound connections
- Following left-to-right, top-to-bottom

### Lesson-by-Lesson Breakdown:

#### **Lessons 1-5: Enormous Turnip**

**Lesson 1:**
- **Reading:** Introduce m, s, p, a sounds ‚Üí Read pages 1-3
- **Writing:** Trace and write uppercase M, S, P, A
- **Journal:** Draw the enormous turnip, trace the word "man"

**Lesson 2:**
- **Reading:** Review m/s/p/a + add n, t, b ‚Üí Read pages 4-6
- **Writing:** Trace and write lowercase m, s, p, a, n, t, b
- **Journal:** Draw the old man, write "man" independently

**Lesson 3:**
- **Reading:** Blending CVC words (man, sat, mat) ‚Üí Read pages 7-9
- **Writing:** Practice writing m-a-n, s-a-t, p-a-t
- **Journal:** Draw someone pulling the turnip, trace "sat"

**Lesson 4:**
- **Reading:** Review all sounds learned ‚Üí Read pages 10-12
- **Writing:** Write CVC words from dictation (say "mat," they write it)
- **Journal:** Draw the turnip coming out! Write "up"

**Lesson 5:**
- **Reading:** Finish story + celebration!
- **Writing:** Write 3 CVC words independently
- **Journal:** Draw your favorite part, label with one word

## Summary: Spiral Learning in Action

### How Phases Overlap:

**Week 1:** Phase 1 (letter formation)  
**Week 3:** Phase 1 + Phase 2 starting (all letters)  
**Week 7:** Phase 2 + Phase 3 starting (words)  
**Week 13:** Phase 3 + Phase 4 starting (sentences)  
**Week 20:** Phase 4 + Phase 5 starting (paragraphs)  
**Week 25+:** All phases active! (reviewing letters while writing multi-paragraph pieces)

### The Beauty of Spiral Learning:

- **Lesson 5:** Writing individual letters
- **Lesson 50:** Writing words with those letters
- **Lesson 100:** Writing sentences with those words
- **Lesson 150:** Writing paragraphs with those sentences
- **Lesson 180:** Writing complex narratives!

**Each skill builds on the last, and NOTHING is left behind!** üåÄ

## Implementation Notes:

### Daily 30-Minute Block Structure:

**Reading Session (15 mins):**
1. Review (2 mins) - Previous patterns/words
2. Phonics Lesson (4 mins) - New concept
3. Story Reading (7 mins) - Chunk of story
4. Quick Check (2 mins) - 1-2 questions

**Writing Session (15 mins):**
1. Mini-Lesson (5 mins) - Writing skill for today's phase
2. Guided Practice (7 mins) - Student writes with support
3. Share/Wrap (3 mins) - Read aloud, quick feedback

### Journal Time:
Can be during writing session OR separate (depending on day type)

### Assessment:
Built into journal - teacher reviews daily to track progress!
