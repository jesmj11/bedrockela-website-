## Benefits of This Expanded Approach

### âœ… More Practice Per Skill
- Kids get 2-3 stories per major skill instead of 1
- Builds confidence through repetition

### âœ… Variety of Story Types
- Long stories (12-17 pages): Billy Goats, Three Pigs, Goldilocks
- Medium stories (8-10 pages): Gingerbread Man, Shoemaker, Town/Country Mouse
- Short stories (3-6 pages): Fox/Grapes, Lion/Mouse, quick fables
- Keeps interest high!

### âœ… Different Themes
- Bravery, teamwork, kindness, honesty, hard work, contentment
- Character education woven throughout

### âœ… Skill Reinforcement
- Each new story reviews previous patterns PLUS adds new ones
- Spiral learning (keep coming back to what you learned before)

### âœ… Reading Stamina Building
- Start with short stories (3-6 pages)
- Build up to medium (8-10 pages)
- Finish with longer stories (12-17 pages)
- By lesson 33, kids can handle a 15-page story!
