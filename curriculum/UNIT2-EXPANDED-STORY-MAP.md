## Unit 2 Breakdown with Story Assignments

### **Lessons 34-39: Magic E Patterns (CVCe)**

**Skills Focus:** Silent E makes vowels say their name (a_e, i_e, o_e, u_e, e_e)

#### Story #1: **Stone Soup** (Lessons 34-39)
- **Length:** Medium (12-14 pages)
- **Magic E words:** stone, made, take, came, gave, place, home, bone, hope, name, taste, safe
- **Why perfect:** Cooperation theme, repetitive structure, each ingredient = mini-chapter
- **Character count:** Village people adding ingredients
- **Reading level:** Shared reading

#### Story #2: **The Magic Fish** (Lessons 34-36)
- **Length:** Short (8-10 pages)
- **Magic E words:** wife, life, made, wish, cave, wave, fine, time, nine, five
- **Why perfect:** "Magic" in the title! Wishes theme, repetitive structure
- **Moral:** Be grateful for what you have
- **Reading level:** Shared reading

---

### **Lessons 40-45: Vowel Teams (AI/AY, EE/EA, IGH/IE)**

**Skills Focus:** Two vowels together make one sound

#### Story #3: **The Brave Little Tailor** (Lessons 40-42)
- **Length:** Medium (12-14 pages)
- **Vowel teams:** brave, tail, wait, may, day, say, away, stay
- **Magic E review:** made, came, safe, save, brave, name
- **Why perfect:** Clever hero, exciting adventures, "Seven at one blow!" refrain
- **Reading level:** Shared reading

#### Story #4: **The Ugly Duckling** (Lessons 43-45)
- **Length:** Medium-Long (14-16 pages)
- **Vowel teams:** see, been, feed, beak, peeked, sweet, feet, seem, mean, each,reach
- **Focus:** EE/EA words heavily featured
- **Why perfect:** Emotional story, character development, perfect for expression practice
- **Theme:** Acceptance, patience, growth
- **Reading level:** Shared reading â†’ Independent

- **Reading level:** Independent â†’ Advanced reading
