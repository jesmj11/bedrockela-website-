## Key Concepts

### Phonogram Sequence
1. m, s, p, a (Lesson 1)
2. n, t, b, j (Lesson 2)
3. g, d, c, y (Lesson 4)
4. h, k, r (Lesson 6)
5. i, v, f, z (Lesson 8)
6. o, l, w (Lesson 10)
7. u (Lesson 12)
8. e (Lesson 14)
9. qu, x (Lesson 16)

### Consonant Teams
- th (both sounds) - Lesson 18
- sh - Lesson 20
- ch (first sound) - Lesson 22

### Blends
- Final blends - Lesson 24
- Initial blends - Lesson 26

### Double Letters
- ff, ll, ss - Lesson 29

### Leap Words (Sight Words)
- the (Lesson 2)
- a (Lesson 6)
- of (Lesson 10)
- was (Lesson 24)
- to (Lesson 26)
- said, I (Lesson 29)
- or, for, no (Lesson 31)

## Comprehension Skills Covered
- Periods and exclamation points (Lesson 3)
- Words with two meanings (Lesson 5)
- Comprehension activities (Lessons 7, 11)
- Punctuation practice (Lesson 9)
- Character's point of view (Lesson 13)
- Story sequencing (Lesson 15)
- Creating silly sentences (Lesson 17)
- Character motivation (Lesson 19)
- Story setting (Lesson 21)
- Retelling with illustrations (Lesson 23)
- Matching text to illustrations (Lesson 25)
- Onomatopoeia (Lesson 27)
- Following recipe instructions (Lesson 30)
- Main conflict (Lesson 33)
