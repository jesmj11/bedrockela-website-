## Implementation Notes

**Pacing:**
- Standard: 1 lesson per day (180 school days)
- Flexible: Can adjust based on student mastery
- Review days built into curriculum

**Differentiation:**
- Struggling readers: Spend extra time in Units 1-2
- Advanced readers: Accelerate through review lessons
- All students should complete foundational phonics (Lessons 1-75)

**Materials Needed:**
- Audio recordings (Billy the Goat's voice)
- Interactive practice activities (digital)
- Student progress tracking
- Parent/teacher dashboard

**BedrockELA Features:**
- Billy the Goat guides every lesson
- Mountain climbing theme throughout
- Gamification with badges & achievements
- Progress visible on mountain trail map
- Practice activities adapt to student performance
