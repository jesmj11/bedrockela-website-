## ğŸ“‹ Lesson Structure (Every Day)

Each 40-55 minute lesson includes:

1. **Welcome & Hook** (2 mins)
2. **Before You Read** (3 mins) - Activation, predictions
3. **Reading** (15-20 mins) - Chapter or passage
4. **Word of the Day** (2 mins) - Vocabulary in context
5. **Check Your Understanding** (10 mins) - Multiple choice + text evidence questions
6. **Reading Journal** (10 mins) - Daily writing (2-3 sentences building to paragraphs)
7. **Wrap-up** (3 mins) - Accomplishments + tomorrow's preview
