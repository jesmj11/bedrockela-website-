## Fable 1: The Lion and the Mouse *(Review from Unit 1!)*
A lion caught a mouse. "Please let me go!" squeaked the mouse. "I'll help you someday!" The lion laughed but let him go. Later, the lion was trapped in a net. The mouse chewed through the ropes and freed him! **Lesson: Even the smallest can help the biggest!**

## Fable 2: The Fox and the Grapes *(Review from Unit 1!)*
A fox saw grapes high on a vine. He jumped and jumped but couldn't reach them. "I didn't want those sour grapes anyway!" he said, walking away. But the grapes were actually sweet! **Lesson: Don't make excuses when you fail. "Sour grapes!"**

## Fable 4: The Crow and the Pitcher
A thirsty crow found a pitcher with a little water at the bottom. His beak couldn't reach! He dropped pebbles in, one by one. The water rose! He drank! **Lesson: Use your brain! Little by little, you can solve big problems!**
