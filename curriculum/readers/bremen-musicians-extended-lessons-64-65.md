# The Town Musicians of Bremen (Extended)
## A Beginning Reader for Lessons 64-65
### A Folktale Retelling For Beginning Readers

---

## For the Teacher or Parent

**Teacher/Parent Note:**  
This extended version of our Unit 1 favorite focuses on **TCH and DGE** consonant teams and **silent letters** (kn, wr). **Bold words** feature target patterns. *Underlined words* are sight words. Theme: Teamwork and courage!

**Target Lessons:** 64-65  
**Phonics Focus:** TCH/DGE endings, silent letters (kn, wr)  
**Key Sight Words:** the, were, said, they, very, could, would  
**Decodable Words:** catch, watch, fetch, match, patch, bridge, ledge, hedge, edge, badge, write, wrong, knee, knew, knock  
**Comprehension Skills:** Teamwork, problem-solving, sequence

---

## How to Use This Book:

1. **First Read:** Use different voices for each animal!
2. **Second Read:** Act out the animals stacking up! Make their sounds together!
3. **Third Read:** Hunt for TCH, DGE, and silent letter words!
4. **Practice:** TCH and DGE come after short vowels. Silent letters are tricky!

## The Story

### Page 1
Once there **was** a donkey. He **was** old and tired.

His owner did not want him anymore. "You are no good!" said the man.

The donkey **was** sad. "I will run away," he said. "I will go to Bremen and become a musician!"

So the donkey set off down the road.

[An old donkey runs away!]

---

### Page 2
As the donkey walked, he saw a dog lying by a **hedge**.

The dog looked tired and sad.

"What is **wrong**?" asked the donkey.

"I am old," said the dog. "My owner says I cannot **catch** rabbits anymore. He does not want me."

[A sad dog by the hedge!]

---

### Page 3
"Come with me to Bremen!" said the donkey. "We will be musicians!"

"I **knew** you would help me!" said the dog happily. "Let's go!"

So the donkey and the dog walked down the road together.

[Two friends now!]

---

### Page 4
Soon they came to a **bridge**. A cat sat on the **ledge**, crying.

"What is **wrong**?" asked the donkey.

"I am old," said the cat. "I cannot **catch** mice anymore. My owner does not want me."

"Come with us to Bremen!" said the dog. "We will be musicians!"

[Three friends now!]

---

### Page 5
The cat smiled. "That sounds wonderful!" she said.

So the donkey, the dog, and the cat walked down the road together.

They **knew** they would make a great team!

[Teamwork makes the dream work!]

---

### Page 6
As the sun began to set, they saw a rooster sitting on a **fence**.

He **was** crowing loudly. "Cock-a-doodle-doo!"

"What is **wrong**?" asked the donkey.

"Tomorrow my owner plans to cook me for dinner!" cried the rooster. "I must run away tonight!"

[A rooster in danger!]

---

### Page 7
"Come with us to Bremen!" said the cat. "We are going to be musicians!"

"I will!" said the rooster. "Let's go!"

So the donkey, the dog, the cat, and the rooster walked down the road together.

Four friends on an adventure!

[Four friends now!]

---

### Page 8
Night fell. The animals **were** tired and hungry.

They saw a light in the distance.

"Let's go see!" said the donkey. "Maybe we can rest there."

They walked to the **edge** of the forest. They saw a little cottage! Light glowed from the window.

[A cottage in the woods!]

---

### Page 9
The donkey looked in the window.

Inside **were** robbers! They sat around a table piled with food!

The robbers **were** counting stolen gold and jewels!

"We must **catch** them!" whispered the dog. "They are thieves!"

"But how?" asked the cat. "There are four of them!"

[Robbers! With stolen treasure!]

---

### Page 10
The donkey had an idea. "Let's **match** our voices together! We will make such a loud noise, they will run away!"

"Good plan!" said the dog.

The donkey stood under the window. The dog stood on the donkey's back. The cat climbed onto the dog. The rooster perched on top of the cat.

[Stacking up! A tower of animals!]

---

### Page 11
Then, all together, they made music!

The donkey: **"HEE-HAW!"**  
The dog: **"WOOF-WOOF!"**  
The cat: **"MEOW!"**  
The rooster: **"COCK-A-DOODLE-DOO!"**

The sound **was** LOUD! VERY loud!

[Make the sounds together! LOUD!]

---

### Page 12
The robbers jumped up! "What is that terrible noise?!" they cried.

They looked at the window. They saw a **strange** creature with four heads!

"A monster!" they screamed. "Run!"

The robbers ran out the door and into the forest! They **knew** they had to get away!

[The robbers are terrified! They run away!]

---

### Page 13
The four friends tumbled through the window. They landed in a **patch** on the floor!

They looked around. Food! A warm fire! Soft beds!

"This is perfect!" said the donkey. "Let's stay here!"

They ate the food. They rested by the fire. They felt safe and happy.

[The cottage is theirs now!]

---

### Page 14
Later that night, one **brave** robber came back. He wanted to **fetch** his gold.

He **crept** to the door. He tried to **knock** quietly.

Suddenly, the animals **woke** up!

[The robber came back!]

---

### Page 15
The cat jumped on the robber's face! She scratched him!

The dog bit his leg!

The donkey kicked him with his back hooves!

The rooster crowed in his ear! **"COCK-A-DOODLE-DOO!"**

The robber ran away as fast as he could! He never came back!

[The animals defended their new home!]

---

### Page 16
The four friends lived in the cottage happily ever after.

They never made it to Bremen. But that **was** okay!

They had each other. They had a home. And **every** night, they made music together under the stars.

**THE END**

[They found a home AND friendship!]

## Back Matter

### Blending Practice
**TCH Words:**  
catch, watch, fetch, match, patch, stretch, scratch, witch, kitchen, latch

**DGE Words:**  
bridge, ledge, hedge, edge, badge, judge, fudge, budge, smudge, ridge

**Silent Letter Words (KN, WR):**  
knew, knee, knock, knot, know, knife, write, wrong, wrap, wrist

**Other Decodable Words:**  
donkey, dog, cat, rooster, road, run, sad, old, tired, home, robbers, safe, brave

### Repeated Phrases to Practice
**"What is wrong?"** | **"Come with us!"** | **"Knew they would!"** | **"Catch them!"**

### Talk About the Story
1. Which four animals became friends? (Donkey, dog, cat, rooster)
2. Where were they going? (To Bremen to be musicians!)
3. What did they find in the forest? (A cottage with robbers inside!)
4. How did they scare the robbers? (They stood on each other and made loud music!)
5. Did they make it to Bremen? (No, they stayed in the cottage!)
6. What is the lesson? (Friends working together can solve any problem!)

### Extension Activities
- **TCH/DGE Hunt:** Find all the TCH and DGE words. Notice they come after short vowels!
- **Silent Letter Detective:** Find KN and WR words. Circle the silent letters!
- **Act It Out:** Stack up like the animals! Make their sounds together!
- **Draw & Write:** Draw all four animals stacked on top of each other!
- **Billy's Connection:** Billy the Goat knows that friends help each other! When goats work together, they can climb anything!

### Character Education
**Theme:** Teamwork and loyalty. Four old, unwanted animals found friendship and courage together. They helped each other and became a family. You're stronger when you work together!

**Discussion:** Have you ever worked with friends to solve a problem?
