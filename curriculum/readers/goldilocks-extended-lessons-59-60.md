# Goldilocks and the Three Bears (Extended)
## A Beginning Reader for Lessons 59-60
### A Fairy Tale Retelling For Beginning Readers

---

## For the Teacher or Parent

**Teacher/Parent Note:**  
This extended version of the Unit 1 favorite focuses on **OO words** (both sounds: "book" and "moon"). We know this story‚Äînow with more OO practice! **Bold words** feature OO patterns. *Underlined words* are sight words. Theme: Respect others' property!

**Target Lessons:** 59-60  
**Phonics Focus:** OO (short sound as in "book" and long sound as in "moon")  
**Key Sight Words:** the, was, were, she, said, they, too, into  
**Decodable Words:** good, wood, look, book, took, shook, food, moon, soon, spoon, room, cool  
**Comprehension Skills:** Sequence, comparing, character actions, consequences

---

## How to Use This Book:

1. **First Read:** Use the three bear voices (Papa: deep, Mama: middle, Baby: high)!
2. **Second Read:** Identify which OO words sound like "book" and which like "moon"!
3. **Third Read:** Child reads the repeated phrases!
4. **Practice:** OO can say /oo/ (book) or /oo/ (moon)!

## The Story

### Page 1
Once upon a time, in a **wood**, there **were** three bears.

Papa Bear **was** big. Mama Bear **was** medium. Baby Bear **was** small.

They lived in a **good** little house in the **woods**.

One morning, Mama Bear made porridge for breakfast. It **was** hot! Too hot to eat!

[Three bears in the woods! Porridge is too hot!]

---

### Page 2
"Let's go for a walk in the **wood** while it **cools**," said Mama Bear.

"**Good** idea!" said Papa Bear.

The three bears walked out the door. They left it open to let **cool** air in.

Soon they were walking among the trees, enjoying the morning.

[The bears went for a walk. The door is open!]

---

### Page 3
A little girl named Goldilocks **was** walking in the **wood** that morning **too**.

She had **golden** hair that shone in the sun.

Goldilocks saw the bears' house. She **looked** in the window.

"A house in the **woods**!" she said. "I wonder who lives here?"

[Goldilocks found the house!]

---

### Page 4
Goldilocks knocked on the door. Knock, knock!

No one answered.

She pushed the door open and **stepped** inside.

"Hello?" she called. "Anyone home?"

The **room was** quiet. No one **was** there.

[She went inside! That's not polite!]

---

### Page 5
Goldilocks **looked** around the **room**. She saw three bowls of porridge on the table.

One big bowl. One medium bowl. One small bowl.

"Mmm, **food**!" said Goldilocks. "I am hungry!"

She **took** a **spoon** and tasted the porridge in the big bowl.

"Yuck! **Too** hot!" she said.

[Three bowls of porridge! She tastes the big one!]

---

### Page 6
Goldilocks tasted the porridge in the medium bowl.

"Yuck! **Too** cold!" she said.

Then she tasted the porridge in the small bowl.

"Mmm! This one is just right!" she said.

She ate it all up! **Soon** the small bowl **was** empty!

[The small bowl is just right! She ate it all!]

---

### Page 7
Goldilocks **looked** around the **room** some more. She saw three chairs.

One big chair. One medium chair. One small chair.

"I am tired," said Goldilocks. "I will sit down."

She sat in the big chair.

"**Too** hard!" she said.

[Three chairs! She tries the big one!]

---

### Page 8
Goldilocks sat in the medium chair.

"**Too** soft!" she said.

Then she sat in the small chair.

"This one is just right!" she said happily.

But then‚ÄîCRACK! The chair broke! Goldilocks fell to the floor!

"Oops!" she said.

[The small chair broke! Crash!]

---

### Page 9
Goldilocks **stood** up. She **shook** herself off.

She **looked** around. She saw stairs.

"I wonder what is up there?" she thought.

She climbed the stairs. At the top, she found a bedroom with three beds!

One big bed. One medium bed. One small bed.

[Three beds upstairs!]

---

### Page 10
Goldilocks **was** **very** tired now.

She lay down on the big bed.

"**Too** hard!" she said.

She lay down on the medium bed.

"**Too** soft!" she said.

Then she lay down on the small bed.

"This one is just right!" she said.

And **soon**, Goldilocks **was** fast asleep!

[She fell asleep in Baby Bear's bed!]

---

### Page 11
Meanwhile, the three bears came home from their walk.

They walked into the **room**.

Papa Bear **looked** at his bowl. "Someone has been eating my porridge!" he said in his big, deep voice.

Mama Bear **looked** at her bowl. "Someone has been eating my porridge!" she said in her medium voice.

Baby Bear **looked** at his bowl. "Someone has been eating my porridge, and they ate it all up!" he cried in his small voice.

[The bears are home! They notice the porridge!]

---

### Page 12
Then Papa Bear **looked** at his chair. "Someone has been sitting in my chair!" he said.

Mama Bear **looked** at her chair. "Someone has been sitting in my chair!" she said.

Baby Bear **looked** at his chair. "Someone has been sitting in my chair, and they broke it!" he cried.

[Someone sat in the chairs!]

---

### Page 13
The three bears climbed the stairs. They went into the bed**room**.

Papa Bear **looked** at his bed. "Someone has been sleeping in my bed!" he said.

Mama Bear **looked** at her bed. "Someone has been sleeping in my bed!" she said.

Baby Bear **looked** at his bed. "Someone has been sleeping in my bed, and there she is!" he cried.

[They found Goldilocks!]

---

### Page 14
Goldilocks woke up! She saw three bears **looking** at her!

"Oh no!" she cried.

She jumped out of bed! She ran down the stairs! She ran out the door!

She ran and ran through the **wood** until she **was** far, far away!

And she never came back.

[Goldilocks ran away! She learned her lesson!]

---

### Page 15
The three bears **shook** their heads.

"What a strange morning!" said Mama Bear.

"I hope she learned not to go into someone else's house without asking!" said Papa Bear.

"I hope so **too**!" said Baby Bear.

And they sat down to make more porridge and fix Baby Bear's chair.

**THE END**

[The bears are safe! And Goldilocks learned a lesson!]

## Back Matter

### Blending Practice
**OO Words (short sound like "book"):**  
good, wood, look, book, took, shook, cook, hook, stood, foot

**OO Words (long sound like "moon"):**  
food, moon, soon, spoon, room, cool, too, zoo, broom, noon, pool, stool

**Other Decodable Words:**  
bears, house, hot, walk, door, girl, bowl, ate, chair, sat, fell, bed, sleep, ran, home

### Repeated Phrases to Practice
**"Too hot! Too cold! Just right!"** | **"Someone has been..."** | **"In the wood"**

### Talk About the Story
1. Where did the three bears live? (In a house in the wood)
2. Why did they go for a walk? (The porridge was too hot‚Äîthey wanted it to cool)
3. What did Goldilocks do in their house? (She ate their food, sat in their chairs, slept in their beds!)
4. What happened to Baby Bear's chair? (Goldilocks broke it!)
5. What did Goldilocks do when the bears came home? (She ran away!)
6. What is the lesson? (Don't go into someone else's home without permission!)

### Extension Activities
- **OO Hunt:** Find all the OO words. Sort them: Does OO sound like "book" or "moon"?
- **Act It Out:** Use three different voices for the bears! Act out Goldilocks tasting, sitting, and sleeping!
- **Draw & Write:** Draw the three bears and the three bowls of porridge!
- **Billy's Connection:** Billy the Goat would never go into someone else's cave without asking! Always respect others' space!

### Character Education
**Theme:** Respect others' property. Goldilocks went into the bears' house without permission. She ate their food and broke their chair! That's not polite! Always ask before touching or using things that aren't yours.

**Discussion:** How would you feel if someone came into your house and used your things without asking?
