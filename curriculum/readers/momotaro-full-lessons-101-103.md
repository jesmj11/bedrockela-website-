# Momotaro (The Peach Boy)
## Lessons 101-103 - Sequence & Hero's Journey

**Japanese Folk Tale**

**Sequence of Events:**

1. **Birth:** An old woman found a giant peach in the river. Inside was a baby boy! They named him Momotaro (Peach Boy).

2. **Growing Up:** Momotaro grew strong and kind. One day he said, "Demons on Demon Island are hurting people. I will stop them!"

3. **The Journey Begins:** His parents gave him millet dumplings. On the road, he met a DOG. "Share a dumpling and I'll help you!" Momotaro agreed. The dog joined him.

4. **More Companions:** A MONKEY asked for a dumpling and joined. Then a PHEASANT (bird) joined too! Now Momotaro had three helpers!

5. **The Battle:** They sailed to Demon Island. The demons were fierce! But Momotaro and his friends were braver! The dog bit, the monkey scratched, the pheasant pecked, and Momotaro fought with his sword!

6. **Victory:** The demons surrendered! "We'll never hurt people again!" they promised. Momotaro took their treasure.

7. **Home:** Momotaro returned home with treasure. His parents were so proud! The village celebrated! Everyone lived happily.
