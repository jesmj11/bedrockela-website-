One day, Stellaluna flew at night and found other bats! Her mother found her! "You're a BAT!" her mother said, hugging her.

Stellaluna brought her bird friends to meet the bats. The birds tried hanging upside-down but fell!

"How can we be so different and feel so much the same?" asked Stellaluna.
