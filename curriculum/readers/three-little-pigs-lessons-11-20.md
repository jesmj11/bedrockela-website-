# The Three Little Pigs
## A Beginning Reader for Lessons 11-20
### A Public Domain Retelling For Beginning Readers

---

## For the Teacher or Parent

**Teacher/Parent Note:**  
This retelling of The Three Little Pigs uses simple CVC words and introduces the consonant teams **th** and **sh** taught in Lessons 18 and 20. The famous "huff and puff" refrain reinforces blending and gives children a predictable phrase to "read" with confidence. **Bold words** are key blending/digraph words. *Underlined words* are sight words to practice.

**Target Lessons:** 11-20  
**Phonics Focus:** All five vowels (a, e, i, o, u), consonant teams **th** and **sh**, /z/ sound of s  
**Key Sight Words:** the, a, of, was, to, said, I, he, you, my, do, go, all, come, no, so, will, then, what, with, his, this  
**Decodable Words:** pig, big, run, fun, den, red, chin, huff, puff, fell, quit, shed, rush, mash, wish, fish, shed, dish, path, bath, that, this, then, them, with, huffs, puffs  
**Comprehension Skills:** Character traits (hardworking vs. lazy), cause & effect, sequence, predictions

---

## How to Use This Book:

1. **First Read:** Read the story aloud to the child. Let them enjoy it! Use different voices for each pig.
2. **Second Read:** Read together. Pause before "I'll **huff** and I'll **puff**" and let the child say it with you!
3. **Third Read:** Let the child try to read with your help. Point to each word. They can read the repeated parts.
4. **Practice:** Use the blending words page at the back to practice decoding **th** and **sh** words.

## The Story

### Page 1
There were **three** little pigs.

A big pig.  
A red pig.  
A wee pig.

"It is time to go!" said the mama pig.  
"Go and make a den of your own!"

[Hold up three fingers! Point to each pig as you read.]

---

### Page 2
The wee pig ran and ran.

"This is fun!" said the wee pig.  
"I do not **wish** to rush."

"I will make my den **quick**!"

So the wee pig got lots of hay.  
**Swish, swish, swish!**  
A den of hay!

[Swish your hands! Swish, swish, swish!]

---

### Page 3
The red pig ran and ran.

"This is fun!" said the red pig.  
"I do not **wish** to rush."

"I will make my den **quick**!"

So the red pig got lots of sticks.  
**Tap, tap, tap!**  
A den of sticks!

[Tap the table! Tap, tap, tap!]

---

### Page 4
The big pig ran and ran.

"I will NOT rush," said the big pig.  
"I **wish** to make a good den!"

So the big pig got lots of bricks.  
**Thud, thud, thud!**  
A den of bricks!

It was a lot of work.  
But the big pig did not **quit**.

[Use a serious, hardworking voice!]

---

### Page 5
**Then** a wolf came!

The wolf had **big teeth**.  
The wolf was bad.  
The wolf ran to the hay den.

"Little pig, little pig, let me come in!"

"No, no! Not by the hair on my **chinny-chin-chin**!"

[Say "chinny-chin-chin" together!]

---

### Page 6
"**Then** I'll **huff**,  
and I'll **puff**,  
and I'll blow your den in!"

So the wolf **huffed**.  
And the wolf **puffed**.  
And he blew the hay den in!

**WHOOOOSH!**

The wee pig ran and ran!  
He ran to the den of sticks!

[Blow like the wolf! WHOOOOSH! Run in place!]

---

### Page 7
The wolf ran to the den of sticks.

"Little pigs, little pigs, let me come in!"

"No, no! Not by the hair on my **chinny-chin-chin**!"

[Say "chinny-chin-chin" together again!]

---

### Page 8
"**Then** I'll **huff**,  
and I'll **puff**,  
and I'll blow your den in!"

So the wolf **huffed**.  
And the wolf **puffed**.  
And he blew the stick den in!

**CRASH!**

The wee pig and the red pig ran and ran!  
**They** ran to the den of bricks!

[Make a crashing sound! CRASH! Run in place!]

---

### Page 9
The wolf ran to the den of bricks.

"Little pigs, little pigs, let me come in!"

"No, no, no! Not by the hair on my **chinny-chin-chin**!"

[All three pigs say it this time! Make it loud!]

---

### Page 10
"**Then** I'll **huff**,  
and I'll **puff**,  
and I'll blow your den in!"

So the wolf **huffed**.  
And the wolf **puffed**.  
And he **huffed** and **puffed** and **huffed**!

But the den did NOT fall!

The bricks were too strong!

[Huff and puff three times! The den stays up!]

---

### Page 11
The wolf got mad.

"I will get in a different way!" said the wolf.

He went up on top of the den.

"I will go in **this** way!" said the wolf.

And he went down the **path** to the chimney.

[Point up! The wolf is climbing!]

---

### Page 12
But the big pig was smart!

The big pig made a big pot of water.  
Hot, hot, hot!

The wolf fell down the chimney.  
**SPLASH!**

"YOWL!" said the wolf.

And the wolf ran off.  
He ran and ran and ran.

And he did not come back.  
Not at all!

[Make a big SPLASH sound! Wave goodbye to the wolf!]

---

### Page 13
The **three** pigs sat in the den of bricks.

"This is a good den!" said the wee pig.  
"This is a strong den!" said the red pig.  
"This is OUR den!" said the big pig.

And all **three** pigs had a **dish** of corn.  
And **they** sang and **they** had fun.

The end!

[Have the child say: "The end!" with you!]

## Back Matter

### Blending Practice: TH Words
**this**, **that**, **then**, **them**, **with**, **path**, **bath**, **teeth**

**TH Practice Sentence:**  
"**This** is **the** path **that** goes **with** **them**."

### Blending Practice: SH Words
**shed**, **rush**, **wish**, **fish**, **dish**, **hush**, **mash**, **swish**

**SH Practice Sentence:**  
"I **wish** for a **fish** in a **dish**!"

### Repeated Phrases to Practice
"I'll **huff**, and I'll **puff**, and I'll blow your den in!"  
"Not by the hair on my **chinny-chin-chin**!"  
"**Swish, swish, swish!**" | "**Tap, tap, tap!**" | "**Thud, thud, thud!**"

### Talk About the Story
1. How many pigs were there? What did each one make?
2. Which pig worked the hardest? How do you know?
3. What did the wolf say to the pigs?
4. Why couldn't the wolf blow down the brick den?
5. What happened to the wolf at the end?
6. What lesson did the wee pig and red pig learn?

### Extension Activities
- **TH & SH Sound Hunt:** Find all the words with **th** and **sh** in the story. Make two lists!
- **Act It Out:** Be the pigs! Build pretend dens with pillows. Take turns being the wolf!
- **Three Houses Craft:** Draw or build the three dens (hay, sticks, bricks). Which is strongest?
- **Character Discussion:** Was the big pig bossy or just smart? How would you build your den?
- **Billy's Connection:** Billy the Goat also met a mean creature under a bridge! How is the troll like the wolf? How is he different?
