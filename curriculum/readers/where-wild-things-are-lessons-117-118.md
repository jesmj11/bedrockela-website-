# Where the Wild Things Are
## Lessons 117-118 - Position Words & Emotions

**In** his room, a forest grew‚Äî**around** him, **over** the walls, **across** the floor!

**Position/Location Words:**
- **in**, **on**, **over**, **under**, **through**, **across**, **around**, **into**, **at**, **from**, **away**, **back**, **with**

**Feeling Words:**
- **Angry** (making mischief)
- **Wild** (with the monsters)
- **Lonely** (wanted to go home)
- **Loved** (supper waiting)

**Theme:** You can use imagination to deal with anger, but home and love are best!
