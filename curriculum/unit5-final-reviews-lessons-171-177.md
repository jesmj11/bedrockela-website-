## Lesson 174: Comprehension Strategies Review

### Billy Says:
"You're not just reading words‚Äîyou're UNDERSTANDING them! That's the real magic!"

### All the Strategies You Know:

**1. Making Predictions:**  
Guess what will happen next!

**2. Finding Main Idea:**  
What is the text mostly about?

**3. Identifying Details:**  
What are the important facts?

**4. Sequencing:**  
What happened first, next, then, last?

**5. Cause & Effect:**  
Why did something happen? What was the result?

**6. Compare & Contrast:**  
How are things alike? How are they different?

**7. Making Inferences:**  
Use clues to figure out what's NOT directly said!

**8. Visualizing:**  
Make pictures in your mind!

**9. Making Connections:**  
Text-to-self, text-to-world, text-to-text!

**10. Asking Questions:**  
Who? What? Where? When? Why? How?

### Practice Passage:

**A Surprise on the Trail**

Billy was climbing his favorite trail on Tuesday. Suddenly, he heard a strange sound. "Chirp! Chirp!" He looked up and saw a baby bird on the ground. Its nest had fallen from the tree!

Billy knew he had to help. Carefully, he pushed the nest back into the tree branches. Then he gently put the baby bird back in its nest. The mother bird returned and chirped happily!

Billy felt good. Helping others always made his heart feel warm.

### Comprehension Questions:
1. **Main Idea:** What is this story about?
2. **Sequence:** What happened first?
3. **Cause/Effect:** Why was the baby bird on the ground?
4. **Inference:** How did Billy feel at the end?
5. **Connection:** Have you ever helped an animal?
